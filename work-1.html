PROJECT OVERVIEW

"Serotonin II: The Fragmented Knight".

Inspired by the sonic world of Yeule, this collection explores the internal collapse and spiritual resilience of a "Broken Female Knight." Through five hallucinatory emotional states: Anger, Sadness, Indifference, Fear, and Arrogance, the garments serve as a psychological landscape. Utilizing modular draping and custom-engineered prints, the series navigates the boundary between futuristic surrealism and emotional deconstruction, symbolizing the journey of rebuilding a shattered self.
