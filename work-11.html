PROJECT OVERVIEW

The tension between the down jacket and the body; exploring cycles and deconstruction in bag design.

A fashion research project exploring the tension between a puffer jacket and the body — and how that same logic of expansion, constraint, and release translates into bag design. The work investigates cycles and deconstruction as structural principles, examining how garments and accessories both liberate and restrict movement.
