PROJECT OVERVIEW

Exploring the connections between drag queens and world cultures.

A fashion design project exploring the connections between drag queens and world cultures — how performance, costume, and identity intersect across different cultural traditions. The work draws on theatrical staging and the visual language of spectacle to create garments that exist at the crossroads of fashion and performance.
