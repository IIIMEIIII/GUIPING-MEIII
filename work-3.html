PROJECT OVERVIEW

Creating wearable accessories and handbag designs for characters using utopianism as the primary critical perspective.

A wearable bag design project using utopianism as a primary critical lens — creating accessories and handbag designs for imagined characters. Each piece embodies an ideal or aspiration, investigating the relationship between the body, the object carried, and the ideological weight we attach to everyday things.
