PROJECT OVERVIEW

Exploring the relationship between acupuncture in traditional Chinese medicine and the healing of the mind, as well as the relationship between knot design and bodily movement.

A wearable bag and accessory design project exploring the relationship between acupuncture in traditional Chinese medicine and the healing of the mind, alongside the relationship between knot design and bodily movement. Each piece draws on the precision and intentionality of acupuncture — treating the body as a site of both physical and emotional mapping.
